Giving Back

At Sierra West Airlines, we firmly believe it is in our corporate DNA to give back to the communities we serve in an effort to make a positive impact on the world around us. This belief is embedded in our daily operations: including our longstanding partnerships with non-profits Wings of Rescue and Fetch Fido-a-Flight.

Partnering with these organizations allows us to transfer small animals from overpopulated shelters to no-kill shelters. View our slideshow below on some of the amazing trips we have had the pleasure to be a part of.
